## formula [][info-formula]

- **66** bread flour or white wheet flour
- **20** corn flour
- **14** cornmeal

- **2.5** active dry yeast
- **2** fine salt
- **12** honey
- **5** natural peanut butter
- **70** whole milk



## method [][info-method]

_sponge n' dough_
